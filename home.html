REACHING KERALA


Reaching Kerala for tourists is relatively straightforward, thanks to its well-developed transportation infrastructure. Here are some common ways to reach Kerala:

  1. By Air:
    • Cochin International Airport: Located in Kochi, this is one of the busiest airports in Kerala and provides excellent international and domestic connectivity.
    • Trivandrum International Airport: Situated in the state's capital, Thiruvananthapuram, this airport also offers international and domestic flights.
    • Calicut International Airport: Located in Kozhikode, this airport connects Kerala to various international and domestic destinations.
      •
  2. By Train:
      3. Kerala has an extensive railway network, and major cities like Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, and Kozhikode are well-connected to other parts of India. You can reach Kerala by train from major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, and Kolkata.
  3. By Road:
      If you prefer road travel, you can reach Kerala by car, bus, or taxi. National highways connect Kerala to neighboring states, making it accessible from places like Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Goa
